Mediterranean Organics™ Caperberries

Caperberries are the delicious, hand-picked fruits of the caper plant. While capers are typically smaller and used as ingredients in recipes, Caperberries are made to eat right off the stem. They are great as an appetizer on a meze platter! Caperberries are served very similar to the way olives are. They can be served alone in a dish, on top of your salad, or used to garnish your favorite drink. This unique, complimentary appetizer item has a less intense flavor than a traditional caper and are about the size of a grape.
